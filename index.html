Project Kiwi

Project Kiwi is an ephemeral, anonymous, real-time polling platform to deliver popular responses to a user's question within thirty seconds. Post a question and watch the opinions roll in. Now, everyone can pitch in to make your snap decisions on the go for you!

In Brief

In Brief scrapes the internet looking for the most relevant pieces of information to help a user dive right into any conversation, whether it be about recent news, or just a topic in general. We curate Wikipedia articles, news articles from Alchemy, recent tweets, and even population sentiment.

CoupleTones

CoupleTones is an Android application that allows users to set favorite locations for a partner and be notified when s/he enters or leaves a favorite location. It is complete with custom vibration patterns and ringtones, and also utilizes Firebase and GCM for user login and push notifications.

Property Finder

A sample demo iOS app that uses React-native and an public property listing API to search up houses available for rent in your area. It displays details such as price, number of bathrooms/bedrooms, etc.

Receipt Tracker

A little handy iOS app I whipped up for my own personal use that allows me to take pictures of my receipts and sort them based on their timestamp. I get monthly automatic emails from the app containing all my receipts, so I never need to stuff another hardcopy receipt in my wallet again.
